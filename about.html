ABOUT US

Passion. Movement. Community.

Silverstone Dance Company exists to inspire confidence, creativity and self-expression through professional dance education.

OUR STORY

Building Confidence One Step At A Time

Silverstone Dance Company was created to provide a welcoming space where dancers of every age and experience level can grow through movement.

From beginner lessons to choreography, private coaching and Zumba fitness, our studio encourages creativity, discipline and confidence.

Our Mission

To provide quality dance education that inspires confidence, creativity and lifelong learning.

Our Vision

To become one of Abuja's leading dance communities, bringing people together through movement.
