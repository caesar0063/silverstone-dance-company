ABOUT SILVERSTONE

A Community Built Around Movement & Passion.

Silverstone Dance Company is a vibrant dance studio in Abuja offering professional training for dancers of all levels. Whether you're stepping into your first class or refining advanced techniques, our instructors are committed to helping you grow with confidence.

From energetic Zumba sessions to choreography, private lessons, and performance training, we provide a welcoming environment where every dancer can thrive.
